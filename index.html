Who Can Register?

Students, early-career professionals, and innovators.

Follow These Steps To Apply

1

Fill out the application form after clicking the apply now button.

2

Await shortlisting and team formation. Shortlisted members are to be in teams of not less than 4 persons and no more than 6 persons.

3

Join the Hackathon and build impactful innovations geared towards reshaping healthcare.The hackathon is a 3 days sprint

4

In the Hackathon, you are required to build a product for Hydra lab bordered around AI for healthcare and its use cases. Each team is to build prototypes of their product.

5

On the final day of the sprint, the teams are to pitch their products to judges who’ll award marks to the teams.
